Our Story

HUDUMA YA UDIAKONIA

Udiakonia ni huduma ya upendo inayotolewa na kanisa au waumini kwa watu walioko katika hali ya uhitaji, kama maskini, wagonjwa, yatima, wajane na waliokata tamaa, kwa lengo la kuonyesha upendo wa Kristo kwa vitendo. Inamaanisha kumtumikia Mungu kwa kuwahudumia wanadamu wenzetu katika hali zao mbalimbali za maisha, ikiwa ni pamoja na kutoa msaada wa kijamii, kiroho, kiafya, au kihisia. Udiakonia ni sehemu ya msingi ya utume wa Kikristo, ikisisitiza mshikamano, huruma, na haki kwa wote. .

Our Mission

To serve God by serving others through acts of love, justice, and compassion—responding to human need, defending the dignity of all people, and working towards healing, empowerment, and transformation in individuals and communities.

Our Vision

To see a compassionate and just society where the love of Christ is expressed through practical service, and where every person — especially the vulnerable — is treated with dignity, supported in their needs, and empowered to live with hope and purpose.

Core values

We are guided by the following values: LOVE, FAITH, HOPE, and PEACE.

Our Programmes

Children Education

KCA takes on the responsibility of helping children in education, by ensuring that those who drop out return to school and provide them with the needs of school

Women Empowerment

Kunguru Care make efforts to connect homeless children them with their families to get the best care and close attentions, or shelter if reunification fail

Rehabilitation Counseling

This strategy focusto help children who are addicted to drugs, disabled, were treated with acts of violence, to build them mentally to be able to live their dreams

Healthy

Street Children cannot afford medical and food expenses because of poverty, Kunguru Care provide health insurance services to managed kids

Food

We provide food and clean water to starving kids and kids living in centers, food for children is very important because there are cases of children who lost their lives starving

Diakonia Outreach

In cases where reunification of street childen with their families fail, Kunguru care take responsibility of sheltering streets kids with homes or centers and ctothings.
